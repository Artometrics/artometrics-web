**Interviewer:** Jordan, the Persona desk publishes a lot of dynasty reports — Warriors, Patriots, YouTube channels, baseball franchises. Why "dynasty" as a frame?

**Jordan Wells:** Because creative industries love narrative continuity — a team, a creator, a studio, a brand that compounds identity over time. Dynasty is not just wins. It is system design: roster construction, platform algorithm fit, institutional memory. Public data lets us compare the story fans tell to the record that survived.

---

**Interviewer:** How is a persona report different from a culture report?

**Jordan Wells:** Culture desk asks what the market or catalog looks like. Persona desk asks who built the machine and whether their measurable record matches the myth. A founder profile or a coach's tenure can be read through departures data, championship clusters, or audience growth curves — always with the limits stated.
